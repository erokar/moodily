Privacy

Our privacy policy is simple: We do not collect any data. All the data about mood, energy, sleep and notes you enter into the app is saved locally on your phone. None of this data is shared with us or any third parties.

The app uses no ad targeting, data mining, or other activities that may compromise your privacy, and we do not affiliate ourselves with any third parties that do so.
